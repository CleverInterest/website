What is a liquidity provider?

A liquidity provider, also known as a market maker, is someone who provides their financial assets to a DeFi (Decentralised Finance) platform to help trading of crypto assets between all visitors of the platform. We can think of this person as a business partner of a currency exchange. In return they are rewarded with fees generated by trades on that platform, which can be thought of as a form of passive income.


Problem

Investing in DeFi (Decentralised Finance) and depositing your financial assets is a complex process requiring knowledge and efforts - bank transfers, purchasing and transferring of crypto assets, choosing the right trading strategy, configuring and managing your accounts in one or more DeFi platforms.

Solution

CleverInterest manages most of the steps and eases the investment process in DeFi allowing you to become a liquidity provider and earn interest on funds you deposit.
